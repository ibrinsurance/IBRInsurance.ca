My Story

From fixing cars to protecting families.

I grew up watching family members navigate a financial system that wasn’t built for them — complicated paperwork, confusing policies, advisors who were more salespeople than advocates. I knew there had to be a better way.

After earning my engineering degree, I joined Ford Motor Company, where I spent years solving complex problems under pressure — analyzing systems, finding failure points, building solutions that hold up. That engineering discipline shapes how I approach every client file.

When you call 647-745-6850, you reach me directly. Not a call centre. Not an assistant. Me.

Why Independent

No allegiance to any one carrier.

I work with 25+ Canadian insurers — iA Financial, Sun Life, Manulife, Canada Life, Equitable Life, and more. I compare every option before making a recommendation. My only obligation is to find what genuinely fits your family and budget.

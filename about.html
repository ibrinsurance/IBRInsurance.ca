🏭 Our Story

From engineering precision
to protecting your family's future.

Rattan Amol Taggar built his career on precision, trust, and results — first as an engineer at Ford Motor Company, now helping families across Canada secure their protected future as an independent life insurance advisor.

My Story

A path built on purpose,
not coincidence.

I grew up watching family members navigate a financial system that wasn't built for them — complicated paperwork, confusing policies, advisors who were more salespeople than advocates. I knew there had to be a better way.

After earning my engineering degree, I joined Ford Motor Company, where I spent years solving complex problems under pressure — analyzing systems, identifying risks, and finding the most efficient path forward. It was rigorous, detail-oriented work, and I loved it.

But I kept coming back to a different problem: the families around me — hardworking Canadians, newcomers, young parents, small business owners — were either underinsured, over-paying, or simply had no plan at all. I realized my engineering mindset could be applied somewhere far more personal: protecting people's financial lives.

In 2019, I became a licensed life insurance advisor. By 2021, I had earned the iA CBP designation — and have earned it every single year since. By 2025 I had reached the IA Premier Excellence level, and in 2026 I achieved IA Premier Expert — the top tier of recognition within iA Financial Group.

Today I serve 500+ families across 8 provinces. I also founded PunjabiFinance, a community of 175,000+ Canadians — especially millennials and new immigrants — learning to navigate personal finance, insurance, and investments. When you call 647-745-6850, you reach me directly. Not a call centre. Not an assistant. Me.

🌄
Our Vision

A Canada where every family has a plan — and the confidence to trust it.

I envision a future where financial protection is not a privilege for the few, but a foundation for every household — regardless of background, language, or where you're starting from. Every Canadian family deserves an advisor who speaks plainly, shops independently, and actually shows up.

🎯
Our Mission

To deliver independent, expert advice that puts your family first — every single time.

My mission is to combine the analytical rigour of an engineer with the empathy of a trusted advisor — comparing 25+ carriers, reading every clause, and explaining everything in plain language so you can make the best decision for your family with complete confidence.

Drug & Dental Insurance

Individual and family drug and dental plans for self-employed Canadians, small business owners, and anyone without employer group benefits.

📞 647-745-6850
  • Prescription drug coverage
  • Dental care: cleanings, fillings, X-rays
  • Basic and major dental procedures
  • Vision care: glasses and contacts
  • Individual and family plan options
  • No employer required — available to anyone

Who Needs This?

  • Self-employed individuals without group benefits
  • Small business owners and their employees
  • Retirees no longer covered by an employer plan
  • Part-time workers without benefits
  • Anyone who wants to reduce out-of-pocket health costs

What's Included?

Plans vary by carrier, but most include prescription drugs, dental (preventive and basic restorative), vision, paramedical services (physiotherapy, massage, chiropractic), and extended health care.

Cost-Effective Coverage

Individual drug and dental plans start from around $80–$150/month depending on age and coverage level. For families, savings on prescriptions and dental alone often exceed the premium cost.
